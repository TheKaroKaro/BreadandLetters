  <div class="mb-20">
    <h2 class="text-3xl font-bold mb-8 pb-4 border-b">Our Story</h2>
    <div class="prose prose-lg max-w-none">
      <p class="text-gray-700 leading-relaxed">
        Bread & Letters was founded from a simple observation: most businesses spend too much time on repetitive, manual work that could be automated. Having spent over a decade in contact center operations—starting as an agent and growing into leadership roles—I saw firsthand how much time was lost to spreadsheets, email chains, and manual data entry.
      </p>
      <p class="text-gray-700 leading-relaxed mt-6">
        I started building automation tools to solve these problems for my own teams. When a simple eLeave system reduced admin time by 70%, and a quality monitoring tool increased coaching sessions from 44 to 117 per month, I realized these solutions could help many more businesses.
      </p>
      <p class="text-gray-700 leading-relaxed mt-6">
        Bread & Letters is the result—a company that combines deep operational experience with technical expertise to deliver solutions that actually work in real business environments.
      </p>
